Salisbury Steak

Ingredients

For Patties
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1/3 cup of Bread Crumbs
  • 2 tsp Ketchup
  • 1 tsp Mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste
For Gravy
  • 2 packets Brown Gravy Mix
  • 2 cup hot Water
  • 1 tsp Ketchup
  • 1/2 cup Worcestershire

Steps

  1. Combine patty ingredients into a large bowl and mix until they are mixed together
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet on medium heat.
  3. Cook patties on both sides until no longer pink
  4. Move temperature to low
  5. Whisk gravy mix and water together until well blended
  6. Whisk in ketchup and Worcestershire and pour over patties in skillet
  7. Allow the sauce to thicken and warm over a simmer
